About Us

Univents is a free app available to all members of a school or community.

We aim to achieve the seamless integration of all events into widespread knowledge of the audience.

Creating and promoting events can be difficult and may not attract as many people as organizations and clubs would like. Univents is a tool to promote and create events on an scale not seen before on school campuses.

Tabling and making posters cost money and it can be a struggle trying to drawn people's attention. Univents strives to eliminate the pain and costs of promoting events.

Universities, colleges, organizations, schools, and even cities can sign up with Univents to host, promote, and market all of their events.
